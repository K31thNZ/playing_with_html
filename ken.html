Game Rules

Turn Flow: Roll 5 dice to gather resources. Use resources to build developments on the board to earn Victory Points (VP).

  • Roads (1 Wood, 1 Brick): Must connect to existing roads. (First road is free placement).
  • Towns (1 Wood, 1 Brick, 1 Sheep, 1 Wheat): Must be placed on an intersection touching your road.
  • Cities (2 Wheat, 3 Ore): Upgrade an existing town. Doubles VP.
  • Workers (1 Ore, 1 Sheep, 1 Wheat): Place on a tile center. Gives +1 of that tile's resource every turn.

Gold: Trade 2 Gold for 1 of any other resource in the shop.

Winning: The game ends after 30 total turns. Highest VP wins!
